Qu'est-ce que c'est ?

Epic the Musical is a musical written by Jorge Rivera Herrans from 2020 to 2024

What is it about ?

Epic the Musical is a reprise of The Odissey, with some adaptations within the story. It is written from the pov (or point of view) of Odysseus
